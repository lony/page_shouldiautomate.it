Should I automate it?

Although we often do things repetitive, we sometimes do not reflect early enough but after 2-5 repetitions and because of the reflection we ask ourselves: Should I automate the task or just do it once more manually?

The form on this site uses the time and the salary you would need to pay to a person who does the task for you and calculates the costs for both decision paths. As a result, this helps to estimate the break-even of an automated branch and leads to a better decision.
